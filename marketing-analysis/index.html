Marketing Spend Analysis

Analysis of marketing channels to optimize budget allocation and ROI.

Objective

Identify the most efficient marketing channels and propose data-driven recommendations.

Tools

  • Python (Pandas, NumPy)
  • Matplotlib
  • Jupyter Notebook

Key Insights

  • Some channels generate high traffic but low conversion
  • Other channels show better ROI with lower spend
  • Budget reallocation could improve overall performance
